Nature Photography

This picture of the sun was clicked by me on my DSLR recently, in 2020. It was clicked in the morning when a solar eclipse was in effect. To click this picture, I used the special glasses used for directly viewing the sun. It is not at all recommended to view the sun (especially during solar eclipse) with naked eye or click its pictures without proper equipment. The reason is that it can make the person blind and in case of camera, the ultra violet rays can severely damage the lens.

This picture of the sun was clicked by me on my DSLR camera recently, in 2020. It was clicked at sunset, and luckily I was able to get a few more pictures like this without damaging my camera lens.
